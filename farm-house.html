Frequently Asked Questions

Is there an extra charge for footing 5 feet depth?

Yes, SSM will charge extra for footing with a depth of 5 feet.

Is external cladding work for elevation included in the cost?

No, external cladding work for elevation will incur additional charges.

Is there an extra charge for constructing additional area?

Yes, additional construction area will be charged based on the extra square footage.

What about the charges for extra lawn space?

Extra lawn space will be charged based on the additional square footage required.

Is interior work included in the base cost?

Interior work will be charged separately and is not included in the base construction cost.

What is the additional cost for fabrication and mesh work?

Extra charges will apply for any fabrication and mesh work required during construction.

Will the compound wall, gate, or designer gate be included?

No, the compound wall, gate, or any designer gates will be charged separately.

Is lift installation covered in the standard pricing?

Lift installation is not covered and will incur additional charges.

What is the extra charge for using red bricks?

There is an additional cost if red bricks are used during construction.

What are the additional costs for adding a sump?

The additional cost for a sump is Rs.16 per litre.
